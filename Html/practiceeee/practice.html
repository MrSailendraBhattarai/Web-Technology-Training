Himalayan WhiteHouse International College, Putalisadak established in 2001, envisioned as the center of excellence for Engineering, Management and Technology education in Nepal is committed to provide best resources to students and practical based education using state of art technology blended teaching, learning and research environment. Our vision is to be a center of excellence in professional education, research and training. Learn More->

The establishment of Purbanchal University in 1993 was visualized as an extraordinary endeavour by the Government of Nepal to create an academic centre of excellence in the Eastern Development Region of Nepal. Its prime role was outlined to act as a catalytic agent for the promotion of socio-economic transformation in Nepal through quality education appropriate for quality life and sustainable future.
